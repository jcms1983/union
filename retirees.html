RETIREES

Our mission is to serve UFMDC retirees and to promote their welfare. We do this by keeping them informed on matters that affect them and by engaging in other activities to further their interests.

Strengthen Social Security 

Social Security is once again under attack, this time from deficit hawks who want to cut benefits and privatize the program. 

A national coalition of more than 50 organizations—the Strengthen Social Security Campaign—has formed to fight this assault, and we are organizing birthday parties across the country. We need your help to make this happen. Visit Strengthen Social Security for more.
